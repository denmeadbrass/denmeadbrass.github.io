Book Denmead Brass For Your Event

Add the joy of live music to your event with Denmead Brass. We play at all types of events, indoors and outside, including formal concerts, country shows, fetes, community celebrations, beer festivals, formal dinners, private parties and even weddings. A full brass band too big? We can also provide smaller ensembles to meet your exact needs.

Hear Denmead Brass Play

Come and hear Denmead Brass play live at one of our upcoming public appearances. We play music in all sorts of styles from film, shows, pop, jazz, classical, as well as traditional brass band music.

Join Denmead Brass

If you play a brass instrument why not join Denmead Brass? Whether you are an experienced brass bander or are new to banding, whether you have been playing regularly or have taken a break from playing why not come along? Having a Main Band and Training Band you will find the right level of support and challenge at Denmead Brass.
